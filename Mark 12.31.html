    • Moral and Ethical Teachings are Religious teaching or laws of how or why to do something. It gives direction to people of a religious tradition on what is believed to be right and wrong, how to treat others, and why to do certian things.

    Maori

    A teaching in the Maori Tradition is Manaakitanga. This teaching encourages supporting others, showing hospitality and giving. Manaakitanga grows the mana of others and failing to show manaakitanga can hurt others. Therfore it is important for people in leadership to show these qualitites.

    Another teaching is Kaitiakitanga which is gaurdianship of the land and it's resources. This teaches being gaurdian of the gifts and things you are to be a steward of these for the next generation. People in the Maori tradition are encouraged to show Kaitiakitanga and the qualitites of forward thinking and selflessness.

    Christian

    There are lots of Moral and Ethical Teachings in the Christian Tradition include the Gospels which contain the commands of Jesus.

    His teachings say those in the Christian Tradition should always try to think about have our action affect people and make sure we are doing the best for others. Christians should show love, kindness, be compassionate and share. Christians shouldn't lie, hurt people, hate or judge people.

    It is also a teaching from Jesus that the Christian Tradition that the followers should share the Gospel with the teachings in it to all people.
