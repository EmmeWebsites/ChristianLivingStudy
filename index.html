    Charateristics

    Characteristics of a religion are the features or qualities that all religions have. They show the religion's beliefs or ideas.

    5 Characteristics I'm studying for the assessment: Rituals, Beliefs, Narratives, Material Expression and Moral and Ethical Teachings.

    Traditions

    Traditions are the different religions, or denominations within a religion, that express each of the characteristics differently.

    The characteristics hold a different meaning in each Tradition and therefore all traditions have dfferent ways which they meet each characteristic.

    The way a religious tradition expresses each characteristic is determined by what they believe and value.
